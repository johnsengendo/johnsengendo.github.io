About Us

Offshore Innovation empowers Norwegian startups and SMEs to establish and thrive in global markets. Supported by Innovation Norway, we have partnered with leading UK Technology Parks, including the University of Surrey (ICT & Space Technology), the University of Manchester (Medicine), and Aberdeen University (Oil & Gas). Our initiative, "Springboard UK," is celebrated as a benchmark for international business development.
