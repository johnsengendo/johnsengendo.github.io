Offshore Innovation

is a Norwegian registered company which has invested in, and provide consultancy and advise related innovative projects in Technology, Agriculture and Fisheries.

History

Offshore Innovation was established as a UK registered company No. 5751115 in 2015 by Hans Peter Christophersen. Offshore Innovation Ltd. was involved with supporting Norwegian start-up companies looking to UK and global markets. With support by the Norwegian Government through Innovation Norway, five centers to facilitate for the Norwegian start-ups were established in the UK with a focus on ICT, Health and Energy.

About Us

One other key activity for Offshore Innovation Ltd. was to undertake the task as Worldwide sector coordinator for Innovation Norway, the Norwegian government organization to promote business and trade.

Our Impact

During the period 2006 to 2024 Hans Peter Christophersen was engaged with the Norwegian Government’s “Oil for Development” initiative, as an advisor and diplomat supporting developing countries to manage their petroleum resources. Offshore Innovation was reestablished in 2024 as a Norwegian registered company (933 877 604) as a technology consultancy as well as investor in agriculture and fisheries.
