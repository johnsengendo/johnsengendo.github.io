Offshore Innovation

A Norwegian registered company investing in and providing consultancy on innovative projects in Technology, Agriculture, and Fisheries.

Our History

Established as a UK-registered company in 2015, Offshore Innovation supported Norwegian start-ups with a global focus on ICT, Health, and Energy.

Worldwide Coordination

Key activities included serving as a Worldwide Sector Coordinator for Innovation Norway, promoting Norwegian business and trade abroad.

Our Impact

From 2006 to 2024, Offshore Innovation supported the Norwegian Government’s “Oil for Development” initiative, advising developing countries in petroleum resource management.

About Us

Offshore Innovation was re-established in 2024 as a Norwegian-registered company (933 877 604), focusing on technology consultancy as well as investments in agriculture and fisheries.
