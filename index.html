About Us

Offshore Innovation was established as a UK registered company No. 5751115 in 2015 by Mr Hans Peter Christophersen. Offshore Innovation Ltd. was involved in supporting Norwegian start-up companies looking to UK and global markets. With support by the Norwegian Government through Innovation Norway, five centers to facilitate for the Norwegian start-ups were established in the UK with a focus on ICT, Health, and Energy.

One key activity was to undertake the task as Worldwide sector coordinator for Innovation Norway, the Norwegian government organization to promote business and trade.

During the period 2006 to 2024, Mr Hans Peter Christophersen was engaged with the Norwegian Government’s “Oil for Development” initiative, as an advisor and diplomat supporting developing countries to manage their petroleum resources. Offshore Innovation was reestablished in 2024 as a Norwegian registered company (933 877 604) as a technology consultancy as well as investor in agriculture and fisheries.
